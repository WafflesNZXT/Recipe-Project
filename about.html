Welcome to Mom's Kitchen!

Hello! I'm Dure Shahwar, and this website is dedicated to sharing delicious and authentic recipes that I love to cook for my family.

On this site, you'll find a variety of recipes, from comforting homemade meals to exciting new flavors, with a special emphasis on Pakistani Cuisine. Each recipe is tested in my own kitchen and comes with detailed instructions to ensure your success.

Thank you for stopping by! I hope you find something here that inspires you to get cooking.

What You'll Find Here:

  • **Tested Recipes:** Every dish is cooked and perfected before it's shared.
  • **Step-by-Step Instructions:** Easy to follow guides for all skill levels.
  • **Tips & Tricks:** To help you master your cooking skills.

Our Mission

To inspire home cooks around the world to explore new flavors, create memorable meals, and build confidence in the kitchen through accessible and authentic recipes.
